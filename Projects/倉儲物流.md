# 倉儲物流

Start a 20000 square feet stores and wholesale shop

## Establish a physical location

The first step to starting the store and wholesale shop is to find a suitable physical location. Look for a space that is at least 20,000 square feet and is easily accessible to suppliers and customers.

## Obtain necessary permits and licenses

Before opening the store, you will need to obtain the necessary permits and licenses from your local government. This will likely include a business license, zoning permit, and health department inspection.

## Purchase inventory

Once you have a physical location secured and the necessary permits and licenses, you can begin purchasing inventory. Work with suppliers to find the products you want to sell in your store and negotiate pricing.

## Hire staff

As you get closer to opening the store, start hiring staff to help run the business. This may include sales associates, warehouse workers, and administrative staff.

## Market the store

Finally, start marketing the store to potential customers. This may include advertising in local media, hosting a grand opening event, and creating a social media presence.

## 确定实体店的位置

开始开设店铺和批发商店的第一步是找到一个合适的实体位置。寻找一个至少有20,000平方英尺的空间，易于供应商和顾客进出。

## 获取必要的许可证和执照

在开业之前，您需要从当地政府获得必要的许可证和执照。这可能包括营业执照、区域许可证和卫生部门检查。

## 购买库存

一旦您找到了一个物理位置并获得了必要的许可证和执照，就可以开始购买库存。与供应商合作找到您想在店里销售的产品并协商价格。

## 招聘员工

在开店之前，开始招聘员工来帮助经营业务。这可能包括销售人员、仓库工人和行政人员。

## 推广店铺

最后，开始向潜在客户推广店铺。这可能包括在当地媒体上打广告、举办盛大开幕活动和创建社交媒体存在。
